A good personal site does not need constant motion. It needs a point of view.

That is why the theme leans on **typography, spacing, and rhythm** more than animation. The homepage is compact. Lists are plainspoken. Colors are few. The result is something that still feels personal without becoming heavy.

> Static sites can still feel inhabited. The trick is giving them texture, not noise.
